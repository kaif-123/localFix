FAQs
How do I book a service?
›
Select a category from the home screen, choose your service type, and tap Book Now. We'll find the nearest available worker for you.
How is the price calculated?
›
Price is based on the type of service, time taken, and any parts required. You'll see an estimated range before booking.
Can I cancel a booking?
›
Yes, you can cancel anytime before the worker arrives. After arrival, cancellation may not be possible.
Are workers verified?
›
Yes! All workers go through Aadhar ID verification and background checks before being listed on LocalFix.
